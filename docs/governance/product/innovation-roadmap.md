# Innovation Roadmap - Phase 2

## Purpose

This roadmap translates the strategic opportunities into a sequenced product plan for the next growth phase.

## Phase 2 Priorities

| Rank | Story | Theme | Why it matters | Primary Dependency |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | FS-32 | Operational guardrails | The governance plane must be reliable before we scale higher-value features. | Tenant isolation, idempotency, concurrency |
| 2 | FS-33 | Explainable authorization graph | Supports safer approvals, support diagnostics, and market differentiation. | Auth graph engine |
| 3 | FS-34 | Business-semantic access packages | Makes UMS feel product-native for SaaS operations and partner governance. | Authorization catalog, approval flow |
| 4 | FS-35 | Continuous access health | Gives teams a proactive governance posture instead of only point-in-time reviews. | Audit trail, review campaigns, access data |

## Suggested Delivery Waves

### Wave 1

- FS-32: operational guardrails
- FS-33: authorization graph explorer

### Wave 2

- FS-34: business-semantic access packages
- FS-35: continuous access health

## Strategic Outcome

Phase 2 should move UMS from "a strong authorization system" to "a differentiated governance control plane" by making access explainable, business-aware, and continuously measurable.
